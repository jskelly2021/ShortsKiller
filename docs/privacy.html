Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

ShortsKiller respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the ShortsKiller app and Safari extension handle user data.

Information Collection

ShortsKiller does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

How the Extension Works

ShortsKiller operates locally on your device. The Safari extension modifies YouTube web pages in your browser to remove Shorts content and redirect Shorts URLs.

No browsing data is sent to ShortsKiller, the developer, or external servers.

Permissions

The extension requires permission to access YouTube pages so it can hide Shorts-related page elements and redirect Shorts URLs. This permission is used only for the extension's local functionality inside Safari.

Third-Party Services

ShortsKiller does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, tracking tools, or third-party data services.

Data Sharing

Because ShortsKiller does not collect user data, no user data is shared with any third parties.

Security

ShortsKiller does not store user data. This minimizes the risk of personal data exposure through the app or extension.

Children's Privacy

ShortsKiller does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, email support.shortskiller@gmail.com.
