Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

ShortsKiller respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app and its Safari extension handle user data.

Information Collection

ShortsKiller does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

How the Extension Works

The Safari extension operates locally on your device. It modifies YouTube web pages in your browser to remove Shorts content and redirect Shorts URLs.

No browsing data is transmitted to external servers.

Permissions

The extension requires access to YouTube pages in order to function. This access is used solely to modify page content locally within Safari.

Third-Party Services

ShortsKiller does not use analytics, tracking tools, or third-party services.

Data Sharing

No user data is collected or shared with any third parties.

Security

Because no user data is collected or stored, there is no risk of personal data exposure through this app.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions, you can contact the developer via the App Store listing.
